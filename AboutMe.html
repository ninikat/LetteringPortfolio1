About Me



Hello, my name is Nydia Segura and I am a hand letterer! I love all things typography. I am a computer engineer by trade but chalkboards and prints are one of my favorite hobbies! When I am not coughing up chalk dust or inking away, I love to run, bake and tell my dogs they're the cutest in the whole world.
